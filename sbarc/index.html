GUIDELINES FOR REPEATER OPERATION
As licensed amateur radio operators we respect and want to preserve the good name and reputation established by Amateur radio and repeaters. Most of us like to operate with high standards and continue to preserve the reputation of the Amateur Radio service. Many people listen to us. We are judged by our operating habits and by the content of our communications. Most hams operators strive to improve their operating practice. please follow the guidelines set down by the ARRL and can be found in the repeater directory.
