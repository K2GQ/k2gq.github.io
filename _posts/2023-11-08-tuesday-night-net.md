With thanks to our members and friends in the Essex County area, the Tuesday night Roseland net on [146.595 (NE2S/R)](https://www.repeaterbook.com/repeaters/details.php?state_id=34&ID=2845) is becoming a success.  This week we had nine check-ins, a high water mark for the weekly net.

The weekly net is an effective way to communicate about club events, create awareness of the club in our community, and generally check in with each other during the week.  If you haven't had a chance to participate yet, please give it a try.

Special thanks to Maurice, KB2SUL for his excellent work as net control.
