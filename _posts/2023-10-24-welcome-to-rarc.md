Welcome to the Roseland Amateur Radio Club, located in the borough of Roseland, Essex County, New Jersey.

We are a casual club composed of members having technician, general and amateur extra class licensees.  We mentor beginners and tutor for the tech and general exams.  We volunteer, monitor four charity runs each year in this area using UHF/VHF. Our club station is OEM rated.  We are the communications part of Roseland OEM.  We participate in HF contests and do some HF digital modes.   We are very interested in studying and building unusual antennas, one member has years of experience building antennas from LF up into the microwaves.  Currently investigating raspberry pi builds and applications in ham radio; we have a functional SDR2 play for bandwidth analysis.
