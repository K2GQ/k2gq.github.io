Welcome to the Roseland Amateur Radio Club, located in the borough of Roseland, Essex County, New Jersey.

We are a general interest, ARRL affiliated club composed of members holding Technician, General and Amateur Extra class licenses.  We mentor beginners and tutor for the Technician and General license exams.  We volunteer and provide public service oriented communications for charity runs each year in the Essex County, NJ area.  Our club station is OEM rated and we are the communications part of Roseland OEM.  We participate in HF contests and operate HF digital modes.   We are very interested in studying and building unusual antennas, one member has years of experience building antennas from LF up into the microwaves.  Currently investigating Raspberry Pi builds and applications in ham radio; we have a functional SDR2 play for bandwidth analysis.
